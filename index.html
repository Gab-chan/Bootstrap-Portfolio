About Me

I was born in El Salvador in 1996, the capital San Salvador. I am the type of guy know as the otaku loner A.K.A filthy weeb, this means that I love anime and manga. Something else that I really love are video games, specially, indie games. There are many indie games out there and I like many of them, but to give a few examples some of my favorites are Hollow Knight, Celeste, A hat in time, Cuphead, Oneshot, among others. I also really like YouTube and its probably the website in which I spend the most time.
